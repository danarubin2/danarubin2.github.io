Professional Experiences

QA Engineer Intern: Roku

Summer 2021

Over the summer of 2021, I worked virtually on Roku's New Products Team, which is based in Austin, Texas! I was responsible for checking manual test cases as well as creating automated ones. Beyond creating and verifying test cases, I also looked at the logs for tests that had failed in order to determine their cause of failure and report and bugs that were found. Next summer, I will be returning to work with Roku full time as a QA Engineer!
