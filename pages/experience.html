Summer Camp Counselor: Camp Mindy

Summer 2016 - 2019

In my first summer experiences, I served as a first grade counselor at Camp Mindy. Each year, I worked with two other co-counselors and was responsible for making sure that campers stayed safe and had fun. This required me to ensure that campers arrived at activities on time and were following directions. I was also responsible for resolving any conflicts between campers (and, working with first graders, that can be a hefty responsibility at times!). This job strengthened my communication skills with people of all ages, as it required me to work together with co-counselors, children, and parents alike. Although I have moved on from my summer camp days, I look back fondly at my time as a counselor!
