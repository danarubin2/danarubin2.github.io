Welcome to my personal website!

I am a junior Computer Science student and a member of the Chancellor's Science Scholars at UNC Chapel Hill. I am originally from Charlotte, North Carolina and have always had a passion for problem-solving. Whether it was by putting together jigsaw puzzles in my preschool classroom or by taking my first programming course my senior year of high school, I have always found joy in piecing together my own unique solution to the challenges I face. This has led me to UNC, where I study Computer Science with a minor in Mathematics.

I have created this website to consolidate my professional, academic, and personal experiences in a way that looks clean and feels true to my identity as a programmer. It was created using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. I hope that you enjoy learning more about me!
